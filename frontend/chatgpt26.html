What I built

A personal cardiovascular
intelligence engine.

HealthSync analyzes Apple Health data to establish your personal RHR and HRV baseline, detect meaningful drift over time, and generate a plain-English weekly summary via LLMs. All heavy computation runs in the browser — your raw data never leaves your device.
