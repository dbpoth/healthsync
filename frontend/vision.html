How it works
01 Export from Apple Health One tap on your iPhone exports your full health history as XML. on device
02 Parse in your browser Your raw data never leaves your device. All XML parsing runs locally. privacy-first
03 Baseline modeling A 30-day rolling personal baseline is computed for RHR and HRV. local compute
04 Drift detection Each day is scored against your baseline. Sustained deviations are flagged. local compute
05 AI insight 8 numbers are sent to an LLM. A plain-English weekly summary is returned. LLM insight
